ABOUT RUFFY

RUFFY is a rug-proof deflationary BEP20 token on the Binance Smart Chain.

The more $RUFFY you hold, the more tokens you will earn. As you sell your $RUFFY, the value increases due to burning automatically. So it creates an ecosystem that encourages investor to hold their tokens and enjoy the rewards.
